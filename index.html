01 — About

I'm a Computer Science graduate from Ionian University, Corfu, with a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end development. My work spans React single-page applications, serverless architectures, embedded systems, and e-commerce solutions.

My thesis project, TravelInspo, is a full-stack travel destination suggestion web app built with React, TypeScript, Firebase, and the Amadeus flight APIs. It taught me to architect real systems, manage third-party integrations, and build for actual users.

Outside of code, I've spent several seasons working in luxury hospitality in some of Greece's finest resorts, which sharpened my communication, adaptability, and ability to perform under pressure.
